My Projects

"TN Free Bus Travel: Women’s Perspective"

The Tamil Nadu Government's Free Bus Travel Scheme for Women is a transformative initiative aimed at enhancing financial independence, mobility, and social inclusion for women. This study delves into the satisfaction levels of beneficiaries, evaluating key factors such as service efficiency, accessibility, safety, and commuter experience. By analyzing user feedback and real-time challenges, the research provides insights into the scheme's impact on women’s daily lives and explores potential improvements to optimize public transportation policies for sustainable urban mobility.
